To get a dev.to token to a dev.to with USERNAME, ty the following steps:

1. visit "https://dev.to" and login with your account
2. after logged, visit "https://dev.to/settings/extensions"
3. scroll to the end of the page
4. in "Generate a New Key" provide a NAME
5. click in "Generate API Key"
6. click in NAME to reveal your token.
